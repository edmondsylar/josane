Resort Location A quick guide to the resort

Josane Location

Josane Forest Resort is located in the Pearl of Africa, situate 40 Kms from Uganda's Capital, Kampala City. From Kampala City, you take Bombo Road via Bwaise Roundabout at the Northern Bypass and head to Matugga Town at a junction famously known as "Ku Mabanda" where Ssemuto Road starts off Bombo Road. This is a 12 Km distance on a well tarmacked route.

From the Ssemuto Road junction off Bombo Road, you head to Ssemuto Mawale, a 28 Km distance. Ssemuto Mawale is just 3 Kms before Ssemuto Town. The Resort is situate in 2 Kms off the Ssemuto Mawale Junction.
